About GraceLight Church

Rooted in faith. Driven by love. Guided by light.

Our Story

GraceLight Church began in 2002 as a small prayer fellowship in Accra, Ghana. Through God’s grace, it has grown into a vibrant congregation devoted to worship, community service, and spiritual growth. Our mission has always been to be a beacon of hope and faith to all who seek light in their lives.

Over the years, we’ve seen countless lives transformed through the power of prayer, fellowship, and unconditional love. We believe the church is not just a building, but a living body of believers united in purpose and compassion.

What We Believe

Faith in Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as the foundation of our faith and the ultimate expression of God’s love for humanity.

Community

We are called to love our neighbors as ourselves and to serve with humility and joy in all we do.

Hope & Redemption

Through the gospel, we find renewal, forgiveness, and strength to face life’s challenges with faith.
