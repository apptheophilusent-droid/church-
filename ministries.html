Our Ministries

Serving God. Serving People. Transforming Lives.

Discover Grace in Action

Each ministry at GraceLight Church exists to glorify God by meeting the needs of our congregation and community. Whether through worship, outreach, teaching, or support, our ministries are built on love and service.

Youth Fellowship

Empowering young people to grow in faith, leadership, and purpose through fellowship, mentoring, and service.

Music & Worship

Leading the congregation in praise and worship through heartfelt songs that inspire spiritual connection and joy.

Women of Grace

Encouraging and empowering women to grow spiritually and support one another in Christ-centered living.

Men of Purpose

Building strong men of faith, integrity, and service who lead their families and communities with godly character.

Children’s Church

Teaching biblical values to children in a fun, engaging environment that helps them know and love Jesus.

Community Outreach

Extending the love of Christ beyond our walls through charity programs, evangelism, and humanitarian work.
