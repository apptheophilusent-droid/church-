The Twelve Christian Disciplines

These timeless practices strengthen believers and deepen our relationship with Christ.

  1. Prayer: Communing with God in faith and dependence.
  2. Fasting: Sacrificing the physical to focus on the spiritual.
  3. Study: Immersing in Scripture and divine wisdom.
  4. Worship: Expressing love and reverence toward God.
  5. Service: Using gifts and time to serve others selflessly.
  6. Confession: Admitting sins and receiving forgiveness and healing.
  7. Submission: Yielding to God’s will and spiritual authority.
  8. Solitude: Withdrawing from noise to listen to God’s voice.
  9. Simplicity: Living free from material distraction to focus on Christ.
  10. Guidance: Seeking divine and spiritual counsel for direction.
  11. Fellowship: Sharing faith and encouragement within the body of believers.
  12. Evangelism: Spreading the message of Christ to the world.
